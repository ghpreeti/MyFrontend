Obesity Epidemic

Causes

Obesity is influenced by various factors, including genetics, eating patterns, physical activity levels, sleep routines, and social determinants of health (SDOH).

Health Complications

Obesity can lead to various health complications, including high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, and bone and joint diseases.

Solutions

To address the obesity epidemic, a multifaceted approach is necessary. This includes promoting healthy eating patterns, increasing physical activity levels, improving sleep habits, and addressing social determinants of health.
